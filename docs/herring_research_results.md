# Herring Spawn Research Results

This document summarizes public datasets, governmental records, academic research, and community monitoring programs related to Pacific herring spawn events and satellite imagery detection.

## 1. Public Satellite Imagery Datasets & Tools

### Sentinel-2 (A & B) - MSI Sensor
- **Source:** Copernicus Browser (European Space Agency)
- **URL:** [https://browser.dataspace.copernicus.eu/](https://browser.dataspace.copernicus.eu/)
- **What it contains:** 10m-20m resolution multi-spectral imagery. Spawning events appear as bright turquoise plumes in nearshore water.
- **Years covered:** 2015 to Present.
- **How to access:** Web browser, API (Sentinel Hub), or Google Earth Engine.
- **Cost:** Free (requires account registration).
- **Confidence:** High. Primary tool for modern high-res detection.

### Landsat (5, 7, 8, 9) - OLI/TM Sensors
- **Source:** USGS EarthExplorer
- **URL:** [https://earthexplorer.usgs.gov/](https://earthexplorer.usgs.gov/)
- **What it contains:** 30m resolution multi-spectral imagery. Historical record of spawning events via the Spectral Herring Spawning Index (SHSI).
- **Years covered:** 1982 to Present (Landsat 5-9).
- **How to access:** Download via EarthExplorer or analyze via Google Earth Engine.
- **Cost:** Free.
- **Confidence:** High. Essential for long-term historical trends.

### Sentinel-3 - OLCI Sensor
- **Source:** Copernicus / EUMETSAT
- **URL:** [https://www.eumetsat.int/](https://www.eumetsat.int/)
- **What it contains:** 300m resolution ocean color imagery. Highly sensitive to milt reflectance.
- **Years covered:** 2016 to Present.
- **How to access:** Copernicus Data Space Ecosystem.
- **Cost:** Free.
- **Confidence:** High for spectral signature; Medium for spatial detail.

### MODIS (Terra/Aqua)
- **Source:** NASA Earthdata / GIBS
- **URL:** [https://worldview.earthdata.nasa.gov/](https://worldview.earthdata.nasa.gov/)
- **What it contains:** 250m-1km resolution imagery. Useful for broad daily monitoring of massive plumes.
- **Years covered:** 2000 to Present.
- **How to access:** NASA Worldview or Google Earth Engine.
- **Cost:** Free.
- **Confidence:** Medium. Resolution is often too coarse for small nearshore events.

## 2. DFO Spawn Records (Canada)

### Pacific Herring Spawn Index Data (SID)
- **Source:** Government of Canada Open Data
- **URL:** [Open Government Portal](https://open.canada.ca/data/en/dataset/d892511c-d851-4f85-a0ec-708bc05d2810)
- **What it contains:** relative index of spawning biomass, egg deposition survey data (SCUBA and surface).
- **Years covered:** 1951 to 2025 (updated annually).
- **How to access:** [Direct CSV Download (English)](https://api-proxy.edh-cde.dfo-mpo.gc.ca/catalogue/records/d892511c-d851-4f85-a0ec-708bc05d2810/attachments/Pacific_herring_spawn_index_data_2025_EN.csv)
- **Cost:** Free.
- **Confidence:** High. Official government record for BC.

## 3. Washington State Spawn Records (USA)

### WDFW Herring Spawn Index
- **Source:** Washington Department of Fish and Wildlife
- **URL:** [WDFW PHS Marine Map](https://geodataservices.wdfw.wa.gov/hp/phsmar/) / [Puget Sound Vital Signs](https://vitalsigns.pugetsoundinfo.wa.gov/)
- **What it contains:** Documented spawning grounds, pre-spawner holding areas, and biomass estimates for 21 stocks.
- **Years covered:** 1973 to Present.
- **How to access:** Interactive ArcGIS maps (PHS Marine Map) and annual reports.
- **Cost:** Free.
- **Confidence:** High. Lead scientists: Phil Dionne (Phillip.Dionne@dfw.wa.gov) and Kirk Krueger.

## 6. Community & Citizen Science Data

- **URL:** [IMAWG Facebook Page](https://www.facebook.com/people/Pacific-Herring-Spawn-Reporting-IMAWG/100057497467275/)

## 7. Key Academic Papers

### "Satellite Remote Sensing of Herring Spawning Events" (2021)
- **Authors:** Qi, L., et al.
- **URL:** [Geophysical Research Letters](https://doi.org/10.1029/2020GL092126)
- **Contents:** Case study in the Strait of Georgia using Sentinel-2 and Sentinel-3. Defines the spectral signature of milt.
- **Confidence:** Very High (Peer-reviewed journal).

### "From Semen to Satellite" (2025)
- **Authors:** Dallaire, L., & Costa, M.
- **URL:** [ResearchGate DOI](https://doi.org/10.13140/RG.2.2.17669.05606)
- **Contents:** Details the **Spectral Herring Spawning Index (SHSI)** developed at UVic. Analyzes 40+ years of GEE data.
- **Confidence:** High (Technical conference presentation).
